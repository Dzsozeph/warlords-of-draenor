Overview

Gor'ashan stands a top a pedestal, and occasionally calls upon Power Conduits to shield himself during the battle.


During the fight, the team must gather on a platform.

Occasionally, Gor'ashan will stop the lightning bolts and use them to strengthen himself.

To prevent the strengthening, players must jump off the platform and deactivate the active energy converters while avoiding the lightning bolts.

Tactics (Normal / Heroic)

Important

  • Open Rune Conduits to disrupt Power Conduit
  • Move out of Shrapnel Nova to decrease damage taken.
  • Rune Conduits channel power to Gor'ashan, reducing damage taken by 14%, and empowers Gor'ashan to cast Thunder Cacophony.

Tank tasks

  • Move out of Shrapnel Nova to decrease damage taken.
  • Face boss away, swap on Thunder Cacophony or Blades of Steel.

DPS tasks

  • Open Rune Conduits to disrupt Power Conduit
  • Move out of Shrapnel Nova to decrease damage taken.

Healer tasks

  • Watch for heavy AoE bursts; save cooldowns for Thunder Cacophony.

On Mythic difficulty, Gor’ashan gains additional mechanics and faster energy cycling:

  • Extra Power Conduits activate simultaneously, requiring coordination to disable them in time.

  • Empowered Thunder Cacophony now leaves residual lightning zones on the floor that persist for 10 seconds.

  • Static Overload (new ability): Random players are targeted with static charges that explode after 5 seconds, dealing AoE Nature damage. Spread out to avoid chaining.

  • The lightning bolts while climbing the platform travel faster and hit harder — use movement abilities (Blink, Roll, etc.) to reach the top efficiently.

    • Before the fight: Climb up, disable all Power Conduits while dodging lightning.
    • Tank: Face boss away, swap on Thunder Cacophony or Blades of Steel.
    • Healer: Watch for heavy AoE bursts; save cooldowns for Thunder Cacophony.
    • DPS: Spread out, interrupt conduit reactivations, move out of lightning zones.

    • Mythic: Faster lightning, more conduits, and new AoE debuffs — stay mobile!
